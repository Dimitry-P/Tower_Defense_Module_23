**Iteration Goal: Polish + UX Improvement**

Focus on making the game clearer, more satisfying, and visually polished for the player.

### UX Improvements

* Clear and readable HUD
* Easy-to-understand tower prices
* Visible damage feedback
* Better readability of battlefield events
* Clear wave progress display

### Visual Polish

**UI**

* Attractive ability icons
* Consistent button style
* Improved victory/defeat panels
* Readable fonts

**Battlefield**

* Better map background
* Clear enemy paths
* Decorative edge elements
* More varied tiles

**Feedback**

* Shot flash effects
* Hit impact animations
* Tower placement sound
* Improved death effects
